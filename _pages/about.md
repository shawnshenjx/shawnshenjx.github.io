About Me
======

I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the [School of Computer Science](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/engineering/schools/computer-science/) at the **University of Bristol**, and Co-Founder &amp; Chief Scientist of **[Memories.ai](https://memories.ai)** (now OpenInterX). I am a joint member of the [Bristol Vision Institute (BVI)](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/vision-institute/) and the [Bristol Interaction Group (BIG)](http://biglab.co.uk/). I completed my Ph.D. at the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, University of Cambridge, supervised by [Prof. Per Ola Kristensson](https://pokristensson.com/).

My research sits at the intersection of **visual AI, human-computer interaction in extended reality, and long-form video understanding**. I lead a research group of 5 PhD students and serve as **Director of Individual Projects** — the School's largest undergraduate programme (100+ students). In parallel, Memories.ai translates this research into commercial products, serving 45+ enterprise customers with partners including NVIDIA, KDDI Japan, and Samsung.
