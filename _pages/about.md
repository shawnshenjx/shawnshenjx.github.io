About Me
======

I am an Assistant Professor in immersive technologies at the [School of Computer Science](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/engineering/schools/computer-science/), University of Bristol, and Co-founder of [Memories.ai](https://memories.ai). I am a joint member of both the [Bristol Vision Institute (BVI)](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/vision-institute/) and [Bristol Interaction Group (BIG)](http://biglab.co.uk/). I completed my Ph.D. at the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, University of Cambridge, supervised by [Professor Per Ola Kristensson](https://pokristensson.com/).

Research Interests
======

My research focuses on innovating next-generation input techniques and interactive AI applications within Mixed Reality (MR) and video understanding systems. I believe that combining AI and MR can augment human abilities.

About University of Bristol
======

The University of Bristol, located in Bristol, UK — just 90 minutes by train from London — is a top 5 UK university for research quality (THE REF 2021) and ranked 55th in the world (QS 2024). Bristol hosts the Isambard-AI supercomputer, the most powerful in the UK, as part of the new Artificial Intelligence Research Resource (AIRR).
